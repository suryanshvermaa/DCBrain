# DCBrain: AI Platform for Data Centre EPC

**Identity:** DCBrain | ET AI Hackathon 2026 | Start: 2026-06-30 | Team: Nebula

**Problem:** Data Centre EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction) projects suffer from information fragmentation, compliance blind spots, schedule opacity, and knowledge loss.

**Solution:** An AI-powered platform unifying EPC project data using RAG, a Knowledge Graph, automated compliance checking against standards, schedule risk prediction and simulation, and an ensemble of autonomous AI agents.

**Core Features & Scope:**
- RAG Document Search & Knowledge Graph (Cross-document Linking, Citation Engine)
- Automated Compliance Validation (ASHRAE, NFPA, etc.)
- Schedule Intelligence (Risk Prediction, Delay Simulation, What-if Scenarios)
- Procurement Visibility & Intelligence Dashboards
- Comprehensive EPC Intelligence (BOQ, RFI, NCR, Change Orders, Inspection)
- 14 Autonomous AI Agents (Supervisor, Document, Knowledge, Compliance, Schedule Risk, Procurement, Commissioning, Risk Analysis, Executive Copilot, Reporting, Recommendation, Mitigation Planner, Project Health, Data Validation)

**Out of Scope:** Real-time CCTV integration, Full ERP replacement.

**Target Users:** Project Managers, Design Engineers, Procurement Managers, QA/QC, Construction Managers, Directors.

**Tech Stack:** Next.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL, ChromaDB, MinIO, Neo4j 5.x (Graph DB), Gemini 2.5 Flash, BAAI/bge-m3, BullMQ, Redis.
