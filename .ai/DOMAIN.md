# Business Domain (EPC & Data Centres)

This document explains the business domain for developers.

**EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction):**
A contracting arrangement where the contractor is responsible for all activities from design to construction.

**Data Centre:**
A specialized facility housing computer systems. Requires extreme reliability (cooling, power redundancy).

**Procurement:**
Acquiring goods/services from external sources (Vendors). Includes issuing Purchase Orders (POs) and tracking lead times.

**RFI (Request for Information):**
A formal documented process used to clarify ambiguities in plans or specifications.

**NCR (Non-Conformance Report):**
A document that details deviation from expected standards, procedures, or specifications. Extremely critical for Quality Assurance.

**Change Order:**
Work that is added to or deleted from the original scope of work of a contract, which alters the original contract amount or completion date.

**Commissioning:**
Assuring that all systems and components are designed, installed, tested, and operated according to requirements.

**Inspection:**
Quality checks performed on-site to ensure work matches design.

**Vendor:**
A supplier of goods (e.g., generators, chillers) or services.

**BOQ (Bill of Quantities):**
A document in which materials, parts, and labor are itemized and priced.

**Failure Propagation:**
The concept in schedule and system intelligence where a delay or failure in one component (e.g., late chiller delivery) cascades through the dependency graph, impacting subsequent critical path activities.
